## Como usar

1. Acesse: https://aistudio.google.com
2. Clique em **VideoFX** ou **Veo**
3. Faça upload da foto
4. Cole o prompt principal
5. Em "Negative prompt", cole o negative prompt
6. Configure: **5 segundos**, **16:9**
7. Salve o resultado em `C:\Users\mayko\homenagem-mae\revividas\`

## VEO-01 — Foto do Casamento ⭐⭐⭐ PRIORIDADE MÁXIMA
**Arquivo fonte:** `assets/casamento-marlene-pai.jpg`
**Salvar como:** `revividas/v01_casamento.mp4`

**Prompt:**
```
A vintage black and white photograph gently comes to life.
A young Brazilian woman in her early twenties with curly dark
hair pinned back, wearing a white blouse, stands beside a man
as they both look softly down at their hands.

She takes one slow, gentle breath — chest rising and falling
naturally. A barely perceptible tender smile crosses her lips.
Her curly hair sways imperceptibly, as if touched by the
lightest breeze. The man beside her remains still.

The grain and texture of the vintage photograph are fully
preserved. No color is added. Cinematic, photorealistic.
Preserve original faces exactly.

Duration: 5 seconds. Aspect ratio: 16:9.
```

**Negative prompt:**
```
no talking, no mouth movement beyond subtle smile, no added
people, no color added to black and white, no style change,
no cartoon, no aging, no de-aging, no background change,
no new expressions
```
